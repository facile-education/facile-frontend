Accessibility Statement

The operator of the Facile.education solution on behalf of the State of Geneva endeavors, to the best of its abilities, to ensure the accessibility of its internet content to all users, in accordance with the guidelines for accessibility of published web content, established within the framework of standard eCH-0059, as well as within the framework of the World Wide Web Consortium's Accessibility Initiative.

This statement is provisional, a detailed audit will specify the level of compatibility with standard eCH-0059.
